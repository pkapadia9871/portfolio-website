Case Study: MyFlix App

Login

Users can log in to their personalized account using their username and password. They need to have registered with an email as well though.

Landing Page/All Movies

Users can view a list of all movies in the database to view details from, add and delete it from a list of favorites.

Movie Search

Users can search for a movie based on keywords.

Movie Details

Users can view the details of a movie they find, add and delete it from their list of favorites.

Profile

Users have a profile page where they can view the list of movies they have favorited. They can view their email, password and username and change any of them if they want. They can also delete their account.
